turbidsolitude is a paradox much like me. An eternally evolving plethora of emotions that I try to replicate through my art. Art, that is not just an expression but a journey of exploring hitherto unbeknownst facets of my personality. Art that documents evolution of the self and the surroundings, casting aside self doubt and censorship. My worst fears to my moments of unbridled joy, my art is a constant discourse between who I am and who I want to be.

About Me

The essence of my art lies in the expression of my interactions and the emotions they evoke using different depiction techniques. Love, pain, angst, anger and happiness are but actors on the grand stage of my mind with roles dictated by the moment I choose to live in. This theatrical microcosm is my constant companion, whom I have tried to do justice to on stretched canvas cloth and paper using non figurative art closely aligned with abstract expressionism.
